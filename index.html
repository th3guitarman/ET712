Musician ~ Developer ~ Artist

 My name is Aaeron, and I'm an artist in more ways than one. For the past 10 years, music has been my life. As a guitarist and composer, I've always enjoyed creating and communicating ideas in different languages.
 Recently though, I've been composing more code than music. I had always been interested in computer logic, but since learning languages like C++, Java, and more recently HTML/CSS/Javascript, I've glimpsed the nigh unlimited potential and possibility availed to programmers. Employing code to solve problems is artistic and creative, and creating interesting design elements to capture attention is thought-provoking fun.
 I look forward to every opportunity to take a new challenge and, essentially, create yet another new piece of art.
