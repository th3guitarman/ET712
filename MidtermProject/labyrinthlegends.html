Our History

Here before you stand ten legendary heroes whose efforts brought the whole continent of Valm (formerly Valentia) from the brink of total destruction. With their combined strength and tenacity, these youth thwarted the heretic Duma Faifthful, united the wartorn country, and liberated humanity from the reign of a fallen god.

After completing a defensive conquest of Valentia in a high stakes war for everyone’s survival, our heroes descend into the dark of Duma tower. They faced off against hordes of Risen/Undead and constructs, a corrupted Rigelian General, Jedah - archbishop of the Duma Faithful, and Duma - the Fell God - himself. Emerging triumphant, our heroes prepare to face a new threat: Thabes Labyrinth...

Our Heroes

Lucina, Legacy of the Hero King

Age: 17

Hometown: Ylisse

Weapon: Falchion

Vocation: Lord

Rating: -

Bio: Lucina is a strong willed and driven woman who is determined to alter the dark future she came from. She values her comrades deeply and has a sense of justice. Due to the countless deaths and horrific events of the future, she dislikes any form of death, even "noble" ones.
Her main motivations come from her love for her parents, especially Chrom. Lucina is willing to do whatever it takes to see that Chrom stays alive, even if it means going against her wish for no needless deaths. Nevertheless, she is obedient to Chrom and puts her trust in her father's judgements, showing a strong bond of trust with her father.
